Quality Candidates

We believe that discovering the perfect candidate is a very human endeavor. We are all candidates at some point in our career.

We believe that by developing a better approach for Hiring Managers to collaborate with all the best Recruiters, we can identify the perfect candidate.

HireSeat tears down the adversarial conditions between Hiring Managers, Recruiters and Candidates to develop a collaborative approach through Accountability, Transparency and Fair Competition.

Accountability, Transparency
and Fair Competition

Accountability

HireSeat has a built-in feedback loop that scores each recruiter based on the hiring managers feedback.

Transparency

HireSeat’s feedback loop provides all recruiters access to the feedback of all competing recruiter’s candidates.

Competition

Since HireSeat is open to all recruiters, fair transparent competition delivers the best candidates faster.
