HIRE SEAT Auction Rules
  • • The HireSeat Auction is for opportunities for recruiters to have their candidate interviewed and not for guaranteed hiring.

  • • Employers decide how many interview seats will be auctioned off for each job.

  • • The highest bids will have their candidates interviewed. If there are 3 seats, the 3 highest bids will have their candidate interviewed.

  • • The first recruiter to submit a candidate owns that candidate for that job role

  • • Only one candidate can be submitted by each Recruiting Firm

  • • No need to pay for bids until a candidate is successfully placed
When Bids must be paid…

If a recruiter’s candidate wins the auction and all 3 conditions below are met:

  • • Candidate is interviewed.
  • • Candidate is rejected by the employer.
  • • Employer chooses another recruiter’s candidate from the HireSeat Auction.
When Bids are not paid…
  • • If the employer chooses the recruiter’s candidate (the candidate does not need to accept the position).
  • • No candidate from the HireSeat Auctions is chosen by the Employer.
  • • The Recruiter’s candidate is not interviewed.
